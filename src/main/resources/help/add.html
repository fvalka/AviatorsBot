usage: /add [<valid-until>] [metar] [taf] <stations> Subscribe to weather updates of these station(s) Options: valid-until - UTC time in HHmm format or number of hours metar - Receive only METAR updates taf - Receive only TAF updates Examples: /add loww eddm ... subscribe to METARs and TAFs using the default expiration time of 6 hours /add 1507 metar lowg ... subscribe only to METARs of LOWG until 1507Z /add 6 kjfk ... subscribe to METARS and TAFs of KJFK for 6 hours
